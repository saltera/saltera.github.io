Audioengine HD3

    The Audioengine HD3 speakers do not appear to support a sample rate of 44.1kHz which is the default.

    We therefore need change the sample rate to 48kHz and restart pulseaudio.

    sed \
  -i 's/; default-sample-rate = 44100/default-sample-rate = 48000/g' \
  /etc/pulse/daemon.conf
pulseaudio -k
